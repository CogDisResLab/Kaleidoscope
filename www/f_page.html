About

Kaleidoscope aims to be a bioinformatics pipeline for in silico hypothesis exploration. We curated multi-omics datasets across different databases to streamline the process of in silico data exploration of omics signatures for researchers. We utilized application programing interfaces (APIs) of many bioinformatics tools and databases and made them accessible to users under one platform with a standardized user interface. Currently, Kaleidoscope integrates data from Brain RNA-Seq, BrainCloud, BrainAtlas, BrainSpan, STRING, iLINCS, Enrichr, GeneShot, and GWAS Catalog, as well as over 200 (and counting) disease-related differential gene expression datasets. The application is designed to be user-friendly with a unified user interface, while also scalable by offering the option of uploading user-defined datasets.
